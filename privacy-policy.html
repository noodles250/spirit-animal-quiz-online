Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

Information We Collect

Spirit Animal Quiz Online is committed to protecting your privacy. We collect minimal information to provide and improve our spirit animal quiz experience.

Quiz Data

  • Quiz Responses: Your answers to our spirit animal quiz questions are stored locally on your device only
  • Quiz Results: Your spirit animal result is saved in your browser's local storage
  • No Personal Identification: We do not collect names, addresses, or other personal identifiers through the quiz

Optional Information

  • Email Address: Only collected if you voluntarily subscribe to our newsletter
  • Feedback: Any comments or suggestions you choose to share with us

Technical Information

  • Usage Analytics: Anonymous data about how our quiz is used (page views, quiz completion rates)
  • Device Information: Basic technical data (browser type, device type) for optimization purposes
  • Cookies: Small files to remember your preferences and quiz progress

How We Use Information

Any information we collect is used solely to:

  • Provide and improve our spirit animal quiz experience
  • Send newsletter content if you've subscribed
  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance our service
  • Respond to your feedback or questions

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • Quiz results are stored locally on your device, not on our servers
  • Any data transmission is encrypted using standard web security protocols
  • We do not share personal information with third parties
  • Regular security reviews and updates to our systems

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Clear your quiz data by clearing your browser's local storage
  • Unsubscribe from our newsletter at any time
  • Request information about any data we may have collected
  • Ask questions about our privacy practices

Third-Party Services

Our website may use third-party services for analytics and functionality. These services have their own privacy policies:

  • Web analytics services to understand site usage
  • Content delivery networks for faster loading
  • Email service providers for newsletters (if subscribed)

Children's Privacy

Our spirit animal quiz is designed for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify users of significant changes by updating the "last updated" date above.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or our data practices, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@spiritanimalquizonline.com
  • Through our website contact form
